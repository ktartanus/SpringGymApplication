Weight training volume is the amount of work you perform during each workout. Each rep your perform adds to the total workout volume.
In bodybuilding we assume that weight volume is the most important factor in growth of muscle tissue.

In the periodization model the period of high volume is called the hypertrophy phase.
Muscle hypertrophy means the muscle cells grow larger. If your goal is to create a stimulus to gain muscle you should perform high volume training.
